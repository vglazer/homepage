Research

My research interests are in the area of algorithms and computational complexity.

For my Master's thesis, I worked on security in the Random Oracle Model (ROM), a topic in Cryptography. My main result was to prove that every secure canonical identification scheme — even a "trivial" one — can be converted to a Fiat-Shamir signature scheme which is secure in the ROM. I also showed how to modify an early version of a certain public-key encryption scheme to make it CCA2-secure in the ROM.
